About Prerana English School

Prerana English School was started in 2010 under Spoorthi Trust to provide English-medium education in rural areas. The school follows the State Board curriculum and offers classes from Pre-KG to 8th Standard. We focus on good education, discipline, and moral values.

President's Message
It gives me great pleasure to share my vision of Prerana English School, established in 2010 under the guidance of Spoorthi Trust.

Our school was founded with a strong aim to provide quality English-medium education to children in rural areas. We believe that every child, regardless of background, deserves equal opportunities to learn, grow, and succeed in life. Education is not only about academics, but also about building character, confidence, and values.

At Prerana English School, we focus on holistic development, encouraging students to become responsible, disciplined, and compassionate individuals. Our dedicated teachers, supportive management, and caring environment help students discover their true potential.

We are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future while staying rooted in strong moral and cultural values. I thank parents, teachers, and community for their continued trust and support.

Together, let us shape a better tomorrow through education.
Prakash B PPresident

Teaching Methodology

  • Activity-based learning for primary classes
  • Concept-based teaching
